How Domain Knowledge Works

1. Document Analysis

LLM analyzes the PDF to identify main features, sub-features, parameters, and their relationships.

2. Concept Graph

Builds a hierarchical graph preserving parent-child relationships that normal chunking destroys.

3. Smart Generation

When generating tests, enriches context with related concepts to produce more accurate test cases.

Key Benefit:

Solves the "generic test case" problem by understanding that "Advanced Configuration" → "Parameter Validation" → "Boundary Testing" are related concepts, not isolated chunks.
